What is it?

Road to Hire provides professional training, development and job placement for recent high school graduates in the Charlotte metro area. Our mission is to promote economic mobility for motivated young adults, regardless of their ability to afford college.

Our tech tracks prepare graduates for careers in web design & development or information technology. Students in our coding academy learn both front and back end languages as well as other valuable skills via classroom instruction and hands-on training. Students of our IT academy receive A+ certification and learn the ins and outs of hardware, software, and customer service.
